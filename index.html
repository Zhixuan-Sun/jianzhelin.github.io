About Me

I am now a postgraduate student majoring in computer science at UCAS. My research now focuses on hyperspectral image classification and feature selection. I am also interested in the algorithm about machine learning, computer vision and human computation. I believe that for today's modern computer age, which has given everyone data, lots and lots of data, building intelligent system for data processing combining knowledge from human with machine is with crucial significance.

I work at Center for OPTical IMagery Analysis and Learning (OPTIMAL) where I am advised by Porf. Yuan Yuan, senior member, IEEE and Xuelong Li, Fellow, IEEE. Previously, I got my BE degree majoring in Optical Engineering in school of optical and electronic information, HUST on July,1,2013. I received my second BA degree majoring in English in school of Foreign languages, HUST on July,1,2013 .
